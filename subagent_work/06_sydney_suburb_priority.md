# Sydney Suburb Priority List for ARI (Australian Real Estate Agents Index)

## Executive Summary

This document provides a comprehensive prioritization strategy for indexing Greater Sydney suburbs for the ARI programmatic SEO project. With a constraint of ~500 Domain API calls per day (~4 suburbs/day), we have developed a tiered approach focusing on high-value, high-activity suburbs first.

## 3. Regional Grouping Strategy

### Option A: Process by Priority Ranking (Recommended)

**Pros:**
- Captures highest-value opportunities first
- Builds credibility with premium suburbs early
- Better for SEO momentum (high-value pages indexed first)
- Faster ROI on content investment

**Cons:**
- Geographic jumps may confuse local schema
- Harder to build regional content clusters
- Internal linking less natural initially

### Option B: Process by Region

**Pros:**
- Natural content clustering
- Better internal linking opportunities
- Regional pages can be completed faster
- Easier to build local authority

**Cons:**
- May delay high-value suburbs
- Some regions have many low-priority suburbs
- Opportunity cost of processing lower-value first

### Recommended Approach: Hybrid Strategy

1. **Week 1:** Process Top 10 suburbs regardless of region (highest impact)
2. **Week 2-3:** Group remaining Tier 1-2 by region clusters
3. **Week 4+:** Complete regional clusters before moving to new regions
